Abuse
=================
-	Using Hive as a transformation engine/platform  (Pig, Spark, MapReduce)
-	Using Hive as a RDBMS
-	Not for low latency response/application


Use
================
1.	Transformation (Simple transformation)
2. 	Mapping for quering nosql databases.
3. 	Data warehousing      (Spark, Pig)
4.	Metastore for other sql-to-hadoop tools (Impala, Hawq, Tajo, Drill, Presto)
5.	Backend for Reporting and visualization (Excel, applications, notebooks, qlikview, tableau)
